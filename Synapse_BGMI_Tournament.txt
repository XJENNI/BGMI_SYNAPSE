## 2. Tournament Structure

### Phase 1: Online Qualifiers (6–8 February)

**Format Overview**
- Duration: 3 days
- Matches per day: 5 matches
- Total qualifier matches: 15 matches
- Group structure: Teams divided into groups of 8 (Groups A, B, C, D)

**Daily Match Schedule**
- Match 1: Erangel
- Match 2: Miramar
- Match 3: Rondo
- Match 4: Sanhok
- Match 5: Erangel

**Final Match Schedule**
- Match 1: Erangel
- Match 2: Miramar
- Match 3: Rondo
- Match 4: Sanhok
- Match 5: Miramar
- Match 6: Erangel

**Lobby Pairing & Rotation**
Two groups play together in a single lobby each day. Pairings rotate daily to ensure fair competition:
- Day 1 (6 Feb): Groups A+B vs Groups C+D
- Day 2 (7 Feb): Groups A+C vs Groups B+D
- Day 3 (8 Feb): Groups A+D vs Groups B+C

**Qualification Criteria**
- Top 4 teams from each group (4 groups × 4 teams = 16 teams)
- Final qualification: Top 16 teams advance to Offline LAN Finals

### Phase 2: Offline LAN Finals (14 February)

**Venue:** Maulana Azad Medical College Campus, New Delhi

**Participating Teams:** Top 16 teams from online qualifiers

**Format:**
- Location: On-campus, offline competition
- Maps: Erangel, Miramar, Sanhok, Rondo
- Schedule: Full-day event on 14 February
- Championship: Team with highest total points wins

## 3. Eligibility Criteria

### Player Requirements
1. **Team Composition:** Each team must have exactly 4 primary players and 1 optional substitute
2. **College ID Requirement:** At least 2 players per squad must provide valid ID cards from any recognized college
3. **Account Verification:** All players must play on their own registered BGMI accounts
4. **Age & Consent:** Players under 18 must have parental consent as per BGMI and Google Play policies

### Registration Requirements
1. Complete team information via official registration form
2. Provide all players' in-game names (IGNs) and BGMI UIDs
3. Team captain's contact number and email address
4. Proof of college affiliation (ID cards during finals)
5. Submit valid information; incorrect details lead to disqualification

## 4. Scoring System

### Placement Points (Per Match)

| Position | Points |
|----------|--------|
| 1st Place | 10 |
| 2nd Place | 6 |
| 3rd Place | 5 |
| 4th Place | 4 |
| 5th Place | 3 |
| 6th Place | 2 |
| 7th Place | 1 |
| 8th–16th Place | 0 |

### Kill Points
- **1 Elimination = 1 Point**
- Kills are tracked and awarded immediately

### Total Score Calculation
**Team Total Score = Placement Points (All Matches) + Kill Points (All Matches)**

### Tiebreaker Rules (in order of priority)
1. Total accumulated points across all matches
2. Total eliminations (kills) across all matches
3. Best single-match performance (highest points in one match)
4. Most first-place finishes (Chicken Dinners)
5. If still tied: Organiser decision or coin toss

## 5. Tournament Rules & Regulations

### 5.1 Technical Requirements

**Device & Platform**
- Smartphones and tablets only
- Android or iOS devices
- No emulators or cloud gaming services permitted
- Device checks mandatory at LAN finals

**Account & ID Rules**
- Players must use registered BGMI accounts
- Account sharing is strictly prohibited
- Each player must maintain their own account throughout the tournament
- UID and IGN must match registration data

### 5.2 Match Conduct

**Lobby Entry & Timing**
- Team captains receive Room ID and Password via WhatsApp/Discord
- Teams must join lobbies at least 5 minutes before scheduled start time
- Matches begin on schedule; late arrivals play at captain's risk
- If a team is not present by match start, the match proceeds without them

**Player Substitutions**
- Registered substitute players may replace primary players only if approved by admins
- All substitute players must present ID cards at LAN finals
- No mid-match substitutions allowed

**Disconnections & Crashes**
- Individual player disconnections: Player respawns if possible; no match restart
- Match technical issues: Admins will decide case-by-case
- Multiple team crashes: Entire match may be replayed at organizer discretion

### 5.3 Fair Play & Anti-Cheating

**Prohibited Activities**
- Use of any cheat software (wallhack, aimbot, no-recoil, GFX tools, macros)
- Account sharing or account borrowing
- Teaming with other squads during matches
- Stream sniping or ghosting (receiving live information from streams)
- Abusive or threatening behavior toward opponents, staff, or audience

**Penalties for Violations**
- First offense: Warning + zero points for the match
- Second offense: Disqualification from tournament
- Severe violations: Permanent ban from all Synapse events + reporting to BGMI authorities
- Investigation: Organizers may request screen recordings, POV footage, or account history

**Evidence & Investigation**
- Suspicious behavior can trigger admin investigation
- Teams may be asked to provide POV recordings or screen captures
- Failure to provide evidence when requested will be treated as admission of guilt

### 5.4 Behavioral Standards

**Player Conduct**
- Respectful language at all times
- No abusive, hateful, or harassing speech
- No inappropriate usernames, clan tags, or profile pictures
- Follow all instructions from admins and referees immediately
- Violations result in warnings, penalty points, or disqualification

**Spectator Conduct**
- Audience members must not interfere with ongoing matches
- No lobbying for specific teams
- Respect casters and streaming team
